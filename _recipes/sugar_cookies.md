name: Sugar Cookies
contributer: Unknown
ingredients:
    - 1/2 cup shortening
    - 1 cup sugar
    - 2 eggs
    - 1/2 tsp vanilla
    - 2 1/4 cups flour
    - 1/4 tsp salt
    - 2 tsp baking powder
    - 1 Tbsp milk
directions:
    - Heat oven to 325.
    - In a mixer, blend shortening, sugar, eggs, and vanilla.
    - Stir in remaining ingredients
    - Shape cookies and place on baking sheet
    - Bake for 8 minutes.
