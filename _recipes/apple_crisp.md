---
categories: 
    - Dessert 
    - Baking

name: Apple Crisp
contributer: Unknown
ingredients:
    - 2 cups Apples
    - ¾ cup Brown Sugar
    - ¾ cup Flour
    - ¾ cup Oats
    - ½ tsp Cinnamon
    - ¼ tsp Nutmeg
    - ½ cup Butter
directions:
    - Put pealed and cored apple slices into 8x8 pan.
    - Mix remaining ingredients and put on top of apples.
    - Bake 350 for 30 to 40 minutes.
---
