name: Blueberry Cake
contributor: Grammy Russell
ingredients:
    - ¼ cup Butter
    - 2 cups Sugar
    - 2 Eggs
    - ½ tsp Salt
    - 2 tsp Baking Powder
    - 2 tsp Vanilla
    - 2 ¼ cups Flour
    - 1 cups Milk
    - 2 cups Blueberries
directions:
    - Bake 350 for 20 min for muffins, or 35-40 min for cake.
    - Top with cinnamon and sugar mix.
