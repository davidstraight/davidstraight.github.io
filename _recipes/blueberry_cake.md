Blueberry Cake
By Grammy Russell

¼ cup Butter
2 cups Sugar
2 Eggs
½ tsp Salt
2 tsp Baking Powder
2 tsp Vanilla
2 ¼ cups Flour
1 cups Milk
2 cups Blueberries

Bake 350 for 20 min for muffins, or 35-40 min for cake.
Top with cinnamon and sugar mix.
